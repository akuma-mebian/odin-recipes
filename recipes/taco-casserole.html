Best Taco Casserole Recipe

Description

This taco casserole with ground beef and tortilla chips is very tasty.

Serve this casserole with chips, salsa, and green salad.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 2 cups salsa
  • 1 (16 ounce) can chili beans, drained
  • 3 cups tortilla chips, crushed
  • 2 cups sour cream
  • 1 (2 ounce) can sliced black olives, drained
  • 1/2 cup chopped green onion
  • 1/2 cup chopped fresh tomato
  • 2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Step 1

    2. Gather all ingredients.

  2. Step 2

    3. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Spray a 9x13-baking dish with cooking spray.

  3. Step 3

    4. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat. Cook and stir ground beef in the hot skillet until browned and crumbly, 8 to 10 minutes.

  4. Step 4

    5. Stir in salsa, reduce heat, and simmer until liquid is absorbed, about 20 minutes. Stir in beans; cook until heated through.

  5. Step 5

    6. Spread crushed tortilla chips over the bottom of the baking dish; spoon beef mixture on top. Spread sour cream over beef, then sprinkle olives, green onion, and tomatoes on top. Cover with Cheddar cheese.

  6. Step 6

    7. Bake in the preheated oven until hot and bubbly, about 30 minutes.

  7. Step 7

    8. Serve and enjoy!
