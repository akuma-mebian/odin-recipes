Unbelievable Chicken Recipe

Description

The unusual combination of easy ingredients in this marinade for chicken is fabulous!

Used chicken thighs instead of breasts, and this turned out so well.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup cider vinegar
  • 3 tablespoons prepared coarse-ground mustard or to taste
  • 3 cloves garlic, peeled and minced
  • 1 lime, juiced
  • 1/2 lemon, juiced
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar or to taste
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt or to taste
  • ground black pepper to taste
  • 6 tablespoons olive oil
  • 6 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves

Steps

  1. Step 1

    2. Mix cider vinegar, mustard, garlic, lime juice, lemon juice, brown sugar, salt, and pepper together in a large glass or ceramic bowl. Whisk in olive oil. Add chicken and toss evenly to coat. Cover, and marinate in the refrigerator for 8 hours to overnight.

  2. Step 2

    3. Preheat an outdoor grill for high heat.

  3. Step 3

    4. Lightly oil the grill grate. Place chicken on the preheated grill, and cook 6 to 8 minutes per side, until juices run clear. Discard any remaining marinade.
