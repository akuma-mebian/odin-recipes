Apple Cider Hawaiian Roll Donuts Recipe

Description

Apple cider stuffed Hawaiian rolls that are reminiscent of apple donuts.

Delishious beyond words, warm, sweet, comfort food.

Ingredients

  • 1 (12-count) package Hawaiian rolls
  • 1 cup apple pie filling
  • 2 (0.74 ounce) pouches apple cider powdered drink mix
  • 5 tablespoons butter, divided
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon

Steps

  1. Step 1

    2. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).

  2. Step 2

    3. Separate Hawaiian rolls and cut a 1/2-inch slit in the side of each one. Use a finger or the end of a spoon to create a larger opening and a space large enough to fill each roll with about 1 tablespoon filling.

  3. Step 3

    4. Roughly chop apple pie filling and add to a bowl. Stir in 1 packet apple cider mix. Spoon filling into the prepared rolls.

  4. Step 4

    5. Melt 4 tablespoons butter and brush each roll on all sides with melted butter. Place rolls on a lined baking sheet.

  5. Step 5

    6. Bake rolls in the preheated oven until lightly toasted and golden brown, 12 to 14 minutes.

  6. Step 6

    7. Meanwhile, stir together remaining apple cider packet, sugar, and cinnamon in a shallow dish and melt remaining butter.

  7. Step 7

    8. Brush tops of rolls evenly with melted butter and coat each roll in sugar mixture.
